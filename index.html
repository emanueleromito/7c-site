7C is a travel-wear brand created for a generation constantly in transit.

Today, movement is no longer an exception. We live between airports, train stations, waiting rooms, hotel lobbies, sidewalks and screens. We are always leaving, arriving, or moving towards somewhere else.

7C designs garments that reduce the physical and psychological stress of contemporary mobility.

Each piece transforms solutions from transportation systems into wearable support, creating comfort, protection and calm without sacrificing aesthetics.

7C is not made for the destination.
It is made for everything in between.
