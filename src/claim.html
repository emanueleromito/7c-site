Every 7C garment travels with a small card tucked into a hidden sleeve pocket. Use it to register your piece to your archive — it takes about a minute.

01

Find the card

Inside every 7C piece there is a small interior pocket — usually on the inside of the left sleeve, near the cuff. Inside the pocket you will find a folded card.

02

Open the card

The card carries a printed code and a QR. The code is unique to your garment — its serial, edition number and origin run.

03

Scan the QR

Open your phone camera and scan the QR on the card. It will bring you to a private record page reserved for that exact piece.

04

Sign in to claim

Sign in with your email on the right. New or returning — same flow. We send a magic link, and the garment is added to your archive.
